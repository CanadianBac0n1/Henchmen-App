Welcome to D.U.M.

The Department of Unapologetic Mischief is the global leader in stylish subversion, tasteful mayhem, and moderately villainous consultancy services. We specialize in the unthinkable—and occasionally the inconvenient.

Our Mission

To build a better tomorrow by slightly ruining today. We balance chaos and charisma, and pride ourselves on innovation, ambiguity, and well-dressed disruption.

  • Secrecy—even from ourselves

  • World domination—but make it stylish
