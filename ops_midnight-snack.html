Operation: Midnight Snack

RESTRICTED ACCESS

D.U.M. Internal Document | Project Code: MNS-13

Objective: Destabilize sleep schedules and interpersonal trust through targeted interference with shared refrigeration units.

Strategy: Agents infiltrated residential and workplace environments under the guise of friendly roommates and janitorial contractors. Key activities included:

  • Strategic removal of exact bite-sized portions from leftover meals.
  • Swapping food labels to induce identity-based snack confusion.
  • Installing decoy food items with extremely spicy cores.
  • Triggering passive-aggressive sticky note warfare.

Observed Effects: Sleep deprivation, minor food poisoning, and one office-wide hunger strike (resolved after discovery of agent's cheesecake decoy).

Lead Field Operative: ███████

Status: Concluded. Operation deemed a success with multiple parties now unwilling to share communal fridges.
